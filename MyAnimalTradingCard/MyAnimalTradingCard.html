Cheetah

They are built for speed, with a whip-like spine, long legs, and a long tail that acts as a rudder for sudden turns.

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Speed: 80-130km/h
  • Average Lifespan: 10-12 years
  • Habitat: open grasslands and bushy areas in parts of Africa and the Middle East

Cheetahs have a short tan coat that has small black spots which helps them to hide or be camouflaged. There are no spots on the cheetah's white stomach area. They have a slim body, long legs, a deep chest, and a narrow waist.
